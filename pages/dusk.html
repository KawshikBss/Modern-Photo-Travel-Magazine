Dusk

The early morning sun rising over Dhaka city is absolutely a magical moment eyes can behold. The breathtaking beauty of the dark sky with a little bit Sunlight rising in the sky can fill someone with calmness and tranquility with a view to starting the day ahead. There is no noise from the vehicles outside. Only the singing of the birds and the magical color of the sky procreate a fascinating beauty of Dhaka city.
