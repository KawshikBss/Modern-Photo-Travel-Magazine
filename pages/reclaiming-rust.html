Reclaiming Rust

Trading in scraps one at a time. meaningless abyss, the hell we live on.

Eternity relies on the leftovers exchanged. A tortured existence in fright of resemblance.

Amid the ashes, beauty can be found. Time to make amends, time to put up a fight.

Use everything you can to create something fresh. Rebirth, fulfillment of a long-held flesh.
