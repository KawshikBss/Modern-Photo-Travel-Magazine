Editors Note

Dhaka, the capital city of Bangladesh, is a metropolis characterized by enigmatic and contrasting elements. This location serves as a haven for the manifestation of aspirations, where enchantment gracefully permeates its pathways, where challenges manifest as testaments of fortitude onto each countenance, where the reverberation of liberation resounds, and where the weight of responsibilities is borne with unwavering resolve. Within the context of a bustling city with a population of 11 million individuals, this magazine explores the diverse perspectives and narratives that shape the identity of Dhaka. Every nook encapsulates a narrative, and within its pulsations, the amalgamation of aspirations, enchantment, adversity, liberation, and perseverance harmoniously intersects.
